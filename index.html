Ellie White's Homepage

As Walt Whitman once said, "a blade of grass is no less than the journeywork of the stars," and so are we. As members of a vast and mysterious Universe, I think it is very important to explore our surroundings, and learn what we can from what we discover. For in seeking to unlock the secrets of the Cosmos through science, we in turn come to better know ourselves.
